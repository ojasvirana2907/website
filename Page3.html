Best time to visit

  • The city is suitable to visit throughout the year. However, if you are looking to beat the heat then the best time to visit during summers as the weather remains cool throughout.

    • If you want to participate in adventures such as paragliding and treks then any time but the mid year is fit since the months from June till September is the monsoon period.

  • If you are someone planning to enjoy snow and bonfires then winter is the ideal time of visit.

  • Dehradun is 150 miles away from Delhi, the capital of India and the nearest international airport. The city is well connected so you can get here from any state via bus, train and flight. This is the guide to reach Dehradun
